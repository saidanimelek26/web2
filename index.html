Troubleshooting Guide

AOA Protocol Connection Issues

The error "A transfer error has occurred" typically indicates one of the following issues:

  1. Your device doesn't support AOA protocol
  2. USB debugging is enabled on your Android device (disable it)
  3. Driver conflicts on your computer
  4. The device is already in accessory mode

Step-by-Step Solutions

2. Disable USB debugging

3. Disable OEM unlocking if enabled

2. Try a different USB cable and USB port

3. Check if your device manufacturer supports AOA
